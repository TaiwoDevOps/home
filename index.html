WHAT WE DO

We are a FIXED INCOME investment company and we support our clients in providing end-to-end: 

BONDS

Are Fixed income instruments issued by Debt Management Office (DMO) to raise Long Term funds on behalf of the Nigerian Federal Government thus creating opportunities for individuals, institutions and other corporate concerns locally or internationally to enter into a risk free and fixed interest rate benefit investment over a specified period.

TREASURY BILLS

Are Government guaranteed Short Term debt instruments issued by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN). They are traded at both the primary and secondary market to interested investors both locally and internationally. It is risk free and duration of investment are stated upon issuance.
